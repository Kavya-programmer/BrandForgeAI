Build a complete full-stack AI web application called:

🚀 AI Marketing Agency OS

This is a competition-ready AI SaaS product that generates full marketing campaigns, ad scripts, and AI video storyboards.

---

# 🎯 CORE IDEA

The app is an AI marketing agency simulator.

Users input:
- Brand name
- Product description
- Target audience
- Optional theme/style (luxury, Gen Z viral, corporate, emotional, cinematic, etc.)

Then the AI generates a full marketing package.

---

# 🧠 FEATURES REQUIRED

## 1. AI Marketing Generator
Generate:
- Campaign Idea
- Marketing Strategy (viral plan)
- Ad Script (scene-by-scene video ad)
- Social Media captions (Instagram + TikTok + hashtags)

## 2. Theme Engine (VERY IMPORTANT)
User can select or type a theme:
- Luxury / “Emily in Paris” style
- Gen Z viral TikTok
- Corporate professional
- Emotional storytelling
- Minimal Apple-style ads
- High energy sports marketing

AI output must adapt to the theme completely.

---

## 3. AI VIDEO SIMULATION MODULE
Even if real video generation is not available, generate:
- storyboard scenes
- visual descriptions
- camera angles
- timing (15–60 sec ad format)
- prompts for tools like Runway / Pika / Sora
